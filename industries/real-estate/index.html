IT for Brokerages and Agents on the Move

Real estate teams are distributed by nature — agents in the field, support staff in the office, and a constant flow of transaction documents, client data, and wire transfer communications that attract wire fraud attempts.

Collaboration & Productivity

  • Microsoft 365 setup and ongoing administration for brokerages
  • SharePoint and Teams for transaction document management
  • OneDrive and shared drive configuration with proper permissions
  • MLS and CRM integration support — Salesforce, Follow Up Boss, Dotloop
  • E-signature platform integration — DocuSign, Authentisign
  • Remote and mobile access setup for agents in the field

Security & Data Protection

  • Email security hardening to prevent wire fraud and business email compromise
  • MFA enforcement for all staff — especially for email and financial platforms
  • Transaction data backup and document retention
  • Mobile device management for agent phones and tablets
  • New agent and staff IT onboarding and offboarding
  • Security awareness training focused on real estate wire fraud scenarios
