Build It Right Before It Gets Complicated

Most startups inherit IT debt the same way they inherit technical debt — by moving fast and figuring it out later. The problem is that rebuilding an identity infrastructure or fixing a misconfigured M365 tenant at 40 people is significantly harder than getting it right at 10.

Foundation & Infrastructure

  • Entra ID setup from day one — directory, groups, RBAC, and SSO to SaaS tools
  • Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace — tenant setup, security hardening, and administration
  • Cloud infrastructure on Azure, AWS, or GCP — VMs, networking, IAM
  • Linux server and Docker environment setup and management
  • Laptop provisioning workflow — MDM enrollment, app deployment, security baseline
  • SaaS tool rationalization and license management

Growth & Ongoing Support

  • Rapid onboarding and offboarding — new hire day-one IT ready in under 2 hours
  • Security and access control that scales without becoming a bottleneck
  • Fractional IT Director for early-stage companies without a dedicated IT hire
  • IT roadmap aligned to hiring plans and product milestones
  • Vendor evaluation support for productivity tools, security products, and infrastructure
  • SOC 2 and security compliance preparation as you approach enterprise deals
