Construction IT

IT That Works On the Job Site and in the Office

Construction companies have a unique IT challenge — a mix of office staff, project managers, and field crews who all need access to the same files, plans, and communication tools, from very different environments.

Project Technology Support

  • Procore, Bluebeam Revu, PlanGrid, and Autodesk Construction Cloud setup and support
  • Blueprint and drawing distribution — large file sharing and version control
  • Project document management — RFIs, submittals, and change orders
  • Estimating and bidding software support — Sage, Buildertrend, CoConstruct
  • Field tablet and device setup for superintendents and project managers
  • Job site WiFi and connectivity planning for trailers and site offices

Security & Connectivity

  • Secure VPN access for field staff connecting to office systems
  • Mobile device management for field crews — phones, tablets, and laptops
  • Microsoft 365 for construction team communication, email, and file sharing
  • Backup for project files, contracts, and financial documents
  • Subcontractor and vendor secure file sharing and communication
  • IT onboarding for project hires and offboarding when projects close
