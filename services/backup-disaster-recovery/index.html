Backup & Recovery

A Backup You Can Actually Recover From

The question isn't whether you have backups — it's whether you can restore from them under pressure, at 2 AM, after a ransomware attack has encrypted your primary systems. Most businesses discover their backup gaps the hard way.

Backup Services

  • Automated server and workstation backup — local and cloud
  • Microsoft 365 backup: Exchange, SharePoint, OneDrive, and Teams
  • Immutable cloud backup storage with ransomware protection
  • 3-2-1 backup strategy implementation and documentation
  • Backup monitoring and alerting on job failures
  • Regular backup integrity checks and test restores

Disaster Recovery Planning

  • DR plan design with defined RTO and RPO targets
  • Documented recovery runbooks for common failure scenarios
  • Tabletop exercises and real-world restore testing
  • Business continuity plan (BCP) documentation
  • Cloud-based DR environment setup for critical workloads
  • Ransomware incident response preparation and playbook
