Know What You Have Before You Fix It

Most IT decisions at small businesses get made reactively — in response to an outage, a security scare, or a compliance requirement. An IT assessment gives you the full picture before something forces your hand.

IT Security & Infrastructure Assessment

  • Microsoft 365 security configuration and Secure Score review
  • User access controls, MFA coverage, and privileged account audit
  • Backup status, recovery readiness, and DR gap analysis
  • Network and firewall review — open ports, rule hygiene, segmentation
  • Endpoint patch status and vulnerability exposure
  • Written findings report with risk ratings and prioritized recommendations

Fractional CTO / IT Director

  • Ongoing strategic IT leadership without a full-time hire
  • IT roadmap development — 6 and 12-month planning horizons
  • Vendor evaluation and contract negotiation support
  • Board and leadership team IT presentations and briefings
  • Budget planning and IT spend rationalization
  • Compliance readiness guidance for SOC 2, PCI-DSS, HIPAA-adjacent requirements
