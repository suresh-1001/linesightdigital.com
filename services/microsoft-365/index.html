More Than Just Setting Up Email

Most businesses use 20% of what Microsoft 365 actually offers — and leave the other 80% misconfigured or unused. A properly hardened M365 tenant is one of the most cost-effective security investments a small business can make.

Microsoft 365 Administration

  • New tenant setup, domain configuration, and DNS records
  • Migration from Google Workspace, on-prem Exchange, or other mail providers
