Sound Familiar?

The IT problems that keep Bay Area founders up at night

Most small businesses discover their IT gaps the hard way — after an incident, audit, or outage. Here's what we fix before it becomes a crisis.

01
"We don't know who has access to what."

Former employees still in the directory. Shared admin credentials. No MFA enforced. One compromised password away from a breach.

02
"Our backups exist, but we've never tested them."

Ransomware doesn't warn you before it hits. An untested backup is a hypothesis, not a recovery plan. Most businesses find this out the wrong way.

03
"IT is owned by nobody — and everyone at once."

Your most technical engineer handles support tickets. Your office manager set up Microsoft 365. Nobody owns the environment and nobody can see the full picture.
